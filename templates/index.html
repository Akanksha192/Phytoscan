Phytoscan

Your Farm's First Line of Defense.

We help farmers, gardeners, and researchers detect plant diseases easily using AI. Upload a photo, get instant results, and protect your crops — all in one place.

Why Phytoscan?

With the rise in global food demand and climate change challenges, farmers are facing increasing pressure to protect their crops from diseases. Phytoscan leverages advanced deep learning techniques, particularly CNN, to accurately detect plant diseases from images. This provides timely solutions that enhance agricultural productivity and contribute to food security.
